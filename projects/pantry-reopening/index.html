Pantry Reopening

About the Project

Pantry Reopening is a video that announced the Pantry’s big move in location and rebrand. This video was featured in the Pantry’s new website and revealed the new location on the first floor of the Memorial Union.


The Works

The grand reopening was a media-exclusive event held on the first floor of the Memorial Union. You can imagine how special we felt with Aggie Studios was asked to cover this action!

This was the second video we were doing for the ASUCD Pantry to cover their location move and rebrand. With such a beautiful new space and gorgeous designs, the video practically made itself.
