UC Davis 2019 Give Day is an advertisement highlighting the different funds available for donors to contribute to during Give Day. At a glance, it featured all various units and awards that serve UC Davis students throughout each school year.

This video had a hard limit of 45 seconds max and needed to feature as many of the funds as possible within that timeframe. My main challenge was meeting this limit, while still conveying the impact that each unit and award makes on students at UC Davis.

I decided to do this by selecting shots of each organization in action, as opposed to just signs outside their offices. I chose tittilating music (royalty free track hunts are always a challenge!) that matched the feeling of working on a mission.

And that's exactly what I wanted the viewer to leave thinking. By donating to one of these funds, you too could join the mission to help better the lives of UC Davis students in need.
